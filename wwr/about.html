White Water Rafting Co.

Our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and unforgettable white water rafting Experiences. We believe adventure builds confidence, teamwork, and memories that Last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 1998, White Water Rafting Co. grew from a small group of river enthusiasts into a leading outdoor adventure company. Expanding from local rivers to multi‑day trips, family tours, and corporate events, it emphasizes safety, expert guides, eco‑friendly practices, and thrilling experiences that inspire camaraderie and respect for nature.

White Water Rafting Co. broadened its mission beyond adventure, embracing conservation, eco‑friendly practices, and education. Guides became storytellers and ambassadors, enriching trips with meaning. With innovations like night rafting and cultural tours, the company earned global recognition since its founding in 1998.
